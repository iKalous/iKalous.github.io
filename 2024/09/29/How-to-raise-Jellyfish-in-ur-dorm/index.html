How to raise Jellyfish in ur dorm?

by iKalous

Disclaimer

if ur really unlucky to hurt during the process below due to some mistake, the author would not take any responsibility~

Something before start

It need your patience, and it should take u some time, effort and money to make and maintain it.

then if u have thought it twice, let’s start!

First: what to prepare?

  • there are many shops on Taobao selling the set of equipment for raising jellyfish. Choose one with a high favorable rate would no go wrong~
  • the jellyfish prefer water with proper temperature, take Atlantic Sea Nettle Jellyfish for exmaple, 18-30°C is recommended, so if u have a cold winter, it’s better to buy a heating rod for tropical fish.

Second: build ur own jellyfish tank

(usually there would have detailed instruction in the specification in ur set bought from the online shop, so just follow the steps carefully)

though, I’d like to kindly aware u that don’t touch the jellyfish. What’s more, it’s strongly adviced to prepare serveral containers with wide mouth which can help u a lot when process acclimation.

Third: sth u would worry about

  • how to feed the jellyfish?
    • for this, jellyfish feed on brine shrimp, so u need to purchase brine shrimp eggs and hatch them, then use a pipette to drop the shrimp into the jellyfish tank for feeding.
    • Attention! to hatch the eggs a warm environment is also needed, which is similar to the one for jellyfish.
  • the jellyfish don’t move at the very beginning
    • that’s very common. feed them, and keep the water pump working. after two or three days u would see the jellyfish swimming happily in the tank.

Forth: about the spendings

  • the whole set cost the author about $ 300yuan $, so it’s a kind of expensive.
  • it won’t take u much time to look after the jellyfish, but u have to feed them everyday and hatch the brine shrimps twice a day. Just take it as an online game

At last, some pictures

the author want to share the picture of his own jellyfish~

beautiful right? although it would take u some time & money, but it’s worthy after all: Who can resist a cute little jellyfish that provides immense emotional value?
